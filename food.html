FAMOUS FOOD IN TAMIL NADU

Explore traditional south indian tasty food

DOSA

Dosa is a popular South Indian food made from rice and urad dal (black gram), usually eaten with chutney and sambar.

IDLI

Idli is a soft, steamed South Indian food made from fermented rice and urad dal batter.

MEAL

A vegetarian meal is food prepared without meat, fish, or eggs, mainly using vegetables, grains, pulses, and dairy products.

NON VEG MEAL

A non-vegetarian meal includes foods made from meat, fish, or eggs along with grains, vegetables, and spices.

prawns masala roast

Prawn Masala Roast is a tasty South Indian dish made by cooking prawns with spicy masala and roasting it until dry and flavorful.

IceBiriyani

It is fermented leftover rice soaked in water overnight. In the morning, it is eaten with onion, curd, or pickles.

BRIYANI

Biryani is a popular and delicious rice dish made by cooking rice, spices, and meat or vegetables together.

MURUKU

Biryani is a famous and flavorful rice dish made with basmati rice, spices, and meat or vegetables.

PAROTTA

Parotta is a popular South Indian flatbread made from maida (refined flour). It is soft, flaky, and layered, usually served hot.

MEDUVADA

Medu Vada is a popular South Indian snack made from urad dal (black gram). It has a crispy outside and soft inside, usually shaped like a doughnut.

SAMBAR RICE

Sambar rice is a healthy South Indian dish made by mixing rice with sambar (a lentil-based vegetable curry).

RAGIKALI

Ragi Kali (also called Ragi Mudde) is a traditional South Indian food made from ragi flour (finger millet).

AAPPAM

Aappam is a soft, bowl-shaped South Indian dish made from fermented rice batter and coconut. It has crispy edges and a soft center.

kuzhipaniyaram

Kuzhi Paniyaram is a popular South Indian dish made using dosa/idli batter, cooked in a special pan with small round holes. It is crispy outside and soft inside
