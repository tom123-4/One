Discover Tamil Nadu

1. Chennai

Chennai, the capital city, is a vibrant blend of tradition and modernity. From the historic temples of Mylapore to the bustling IT corridors, it offers something for everyone. It is the cultural hub of South India, famous for its classical music and dance festivals.Chennai, the capital of Tamil Nadu and the "Gateway to South India," is a vibrant coastal metropolis blending rich cultural heritage with modern industry. Key tourist attractions include the world-renowned Marina Beach, ancient Dravidian-style temples, British-era colonial architecture, and a thriving art scene (Bharatanatyam/Carnatic music), making it a significant cultural and historic destination.
